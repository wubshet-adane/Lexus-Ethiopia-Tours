Travelling with Us

Travelling to Ethiopia also means to return to the origin of humankind. With much care we have designed a great choice of fantastic tours through Ethiopia. These trips can be individually adjusted as per your personal wishes. We particularly also offer a wide range of shorter trips that may perfectly be combined with each other.

Our Services

We are a Premium Tour Operator offering a wide range of tailor-made cultural and historic tours, photo safaris, animal- and bird watching trips, desert- and mountain trekking, eco-tourism and family-friendly holidays. You may be assured that your trip with us will be an experience of a lifetime!

Our Goal

Our Ethiopia tours are designed to offer you an exciting glimpse behind the curtain, experiencing history firsthand, observing the splendid diversity of the natural and animal world from a front-row seat, while gaining an insight into the lives of local people. Our goal is not just to meet your expectations but to exceed them every day that you are travelling with us.
